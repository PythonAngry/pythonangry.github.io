第一课

The first day of class

write the name on the blackboard

At the end of the class
ask the students: what is my name? where do i come from?
做到前后呼应
把每个问题作为奖赏学生的机会
To get students say something is a good start

介绍the general content of the class, 本学期学习12个单元，你们会学到关于介绍自己，兴趣，运动等话题, Introduce teaching plan: 4 lessons a week, 每一周半会学一节课，下周会学什么，下下周学什么

Share goals or objectives, my goals for this class include:

  1. Working your way around the book
  2. Getting to know each other

Getting to know each other

Give a mini-speech

  • their name
  • where they are from
  • one other thing
    • to name a favorite
    • to explain something unique they can do
    • to introduce an animal or object to describe personality
    • to tell about dream
      please introduce yourself and say your name, where you are from, and your favorite food.

回答时，要求学生表现得更自信, Please turn to face the class. Turn around so everyone can see you. Please raise your head so we can hear your voice better. Please lift your head and speak a little louder, i can't hear you very well.

要适当肯定学生的努力, lovely or nice voice. Good job, that is very interesting, thank you for sharing.
每周随机抽取一些学生自我介绍，或同桌进行相互介绍

Rules and behavior requests

list rules, these are the rules for our class: 1, 2, 3, 4,...
依据自身要求，期望及所教授的学生年龄

  • To ask permission before leaving to the bathroom, answering questions...但需要要求不要什么事情都向老师征求同意

  • consequences for not following the rules

  • small talk

    能顺带巩固单词与过去时态

Please find/take your seat
please put your mobile phones away/ silence your mp

Begin the class

在课堂热身之后，使用class begin较为合适，可以先打招呼，Hello and begin small talk before class. 同时，时刻记得增加句子内容让自己听起来更像个人，Ok, let's begin today's lesson. Let's make a start.

to get students excited about learing english，表达自己学习经历与感想，以及你对学科的热爱

第二课

点名

When i call your name, you say yes. 用于新学生 yes miss yes sir会更有礼貌
Take the register. When settled.
Let's do/take the attendance.
点完之后询问
Anybody whose name I haven't called? Did i miss anybody (out)?
Who is missing? Is anybody missing? 也可以用away替代missing

当学生迟到，请求允许进教室
never mind/ ok/ sure/ all right/ It doesn't matter.
let's go back to what we were doing. Go and sit down, please.

赞扬学生，yes，very good. 不说you are very good, 不地道。
Oh, brilliant. Yes, great job.
you are very helpful. you've been very helpful.

给出任务
X _so_不要用so做开头 after class, i want you to X
After class, i would like you to...
at home, in your own/spare time, for homework, i would like you to prepare role play in groups and each group role plays one part.

给出指示
please speak louder/up. 不说please speak loudly.
Please speak a bit loudly. Don't speak so loud.

第三课

当学生读错
that isn't quite right. tha's close.
That's close, but the XX sound isn't quite right.
如果学生读得太轻
Let's try it again, but this time make the K sound heavier. Book
重复练习单词
Let't try it again, please.
Let's say it again, three more time.
学习新单词，可以借助以前的单词帮助发音
is this like any other words you already know?
或让他们联想
What other words can you think of with this sound?
is there like any other sounds you can think of in english?

区分长短元音，还是拿Reference words做例子，特别是rhyming words
will - wheel
bill, dill, fill, jill, kill, mill, sill, til sa in until
until until will 让学生练，最后让学生默念until，只读出will

改变口型
tongue 'ue' is silent
the tip, front, and back of the tongue
your tongue can be high or low in your mouth

Lips
make our lips narrow. Narrow lips or wide or open or purse lips.

tongue and lips relation to our teeth
top teeth, bottom teeth
putting our tongue between the teeth.
tongue touching the back or behind the teeth

voicing
把手放喉咙，voice box, put you hand here like me and say aaaaaa
can you feel the vibration?
now keep your hand there and just sigh or exhale
can you feel any vibration? you shouldn't feel any vibration now because of just using air.

when you feel the vibration the sound is called voiced.
when you don't feel the vibration and just use air, the sound is called unvoiced.

make an unvoiced sound where your bottom lip touches the bottom of your top teeth.
what sound is that? f.

how to make the oe as in shoe vowel sound?
relax the tongue, purse your lips and make a voiced sound.

techniques: eliciting mimicry, using reference words, explaining the mouth

第四课

teaching intonation

pitch: how high or low a sound is
high pitch: make my voice go higher; low, go lower
rising pitch: the pitch goes from low to high; falling, high to low
falling-rising pitch: the pitch goes lower then higher
用单词作例子练习，两三个音节的单词更好 library

imitate对小学生有点难，要用他们能理解的单词进行教学
模仿，可以说copy me, repeat after me, say these sentences after me

引起学生注意：pay attention, listen carefully, focus
让同学紧跟老师：repeat after me, after me
可以用手势来模仿语音音调的高低，更形象容易理解，肢体语言很重要！

team1234, group1234
when/after i say 1,2,3, you repeat what i say, repeat what i say

用coke，少用cola，用drink，包含了各种饮料，would you like some drink? better than would you like some tea.
让学生了解不同国家的用语习惯。

整体来说 要礼貌，句子要说完整
要留几分钟预告上课，讨论开始前are you ready? 小组讨论结束，课程结束等
反馈要清楚，哪里有问题，帮助改正。针对的是问题，你的发音 你的XX 而不是你错了

意群停顿，标点符号要停顿，重读高调拉长语核，上文重复或意思重复不再强调
句末，意群末单词要拉长，重读
清辅音前元音短，浊辅音前元音长
